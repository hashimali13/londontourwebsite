Explore London..

London is a huge and expansive city, it has history stretching back to the the Roman times, there's just so much to explore it can be daunting. But don't make the mistake of doing things the usual way, if you go on one of those open top buses you're doing yourself a huge disservice, if you're coming to London or just looking to explore the city, the only way to truly capture and experience London is by walking through the city yourself. This way you can explore and personally see the sights of the city.
