Westminster

Westminster is one of the most visible places in London, its home to Buckingham Palace, the Palace of Westminster, and the British government's Parliament. It's located in the City of London, Westminster has a storied and rich past, the Palace of Westminster was the seat of the royals, all way the up until a fire ravaged the Palace in 1512, after that it became the House of Parliament.
Westminster is known throughout the world as an important historic sight, tourists flock from across the globe to take in the sights, and you can only get the best experience out of it by taking it slow and soaking in all of the experience.

Tower of London

Big Ben

Big Ben is arguably the most iconic sight in London, once you see that clock face and the exterior, you instantly think of London. This 19th century clock tower is quite the sight, the best way to observe this magnificent attraction is from across the river Thames, at dusk.

In 2008 a survey of 2,000 people found that the tower was the most popular landmark in the United Kingdom, next to St. Paul's Cathedral and Tower Bridge, this is the attraction people most associate with this city, so if you really want to experience London in all its glory, go on this tour and I'll guarantee that you won't regret it.
