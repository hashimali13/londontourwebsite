About Us

Who am I?

I have more than 30 years of experience in human rights publishing. International human rights law is also my main areas of translation expertise. Since 2014, I have worked as a freelance editor and translator for a wide range of organisations in various parts of the world. I am also a qualified guide for my home city, the City of London.

I was responsible for the development of Amnesty International publications and the management of the publishing team.

I wrote and edited major reports, briefings, policy primers, magazine articles and campaigning web content. I developed and rolled out house style and in-house training for writers.

I led and project managed publications from the development stages with writers, through to design, translation and publication.

Frequently asked questions.

What sets us apart from other tours?

This tour has a focus on lesser known sites in London, typically a London Tour would heavily focus on sites like Buckingham Palace, this tour however will not visit sites that that. The City of London itself has enough interesting sites on its own.

Are you a certified City of London guide.

Yes I am a certified City of London Guide. Not only am I experienced in guiding tours but I also have lots of translation experience. I was an International Secretariat for Amnesty International for 29 years.

Who is this tour for?

This tour is not only for tourist, this tour is geared towards people who already live in London, but haven't seen all the sights. I am qualified to deal with tourists due to my international experience, but I want this tour to help people properly explore London.
